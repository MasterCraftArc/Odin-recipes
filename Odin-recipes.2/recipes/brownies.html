brownies

Another Family classic!

prep time

    Prep 25 minutes
    Cook 25 minutes
    Additional 10 minutes
    Total 1 hour
    servings 16

Ingredients

    ½ cup white sugar
    2 tablespoons butter
    2 tablespoons water
    1½ cups semisweet chocolate chips
    2 eggs
    ½ teaspoon vanilla extract
    ⅔ cup all-purpose flour
    ¼ teaspoon baking soda
    ½ teaspoon salt
